The '''1902 Guatemala earthquake''' struck on April 18, 1902 at 20:23:50 hrs. It had an estimated magnitude of 7.5 Moment magnitude scale|M<sub>w</sub> and an epicenter depth of approximately 25&nbsp;km. The quake had a duration of 1 to 2 minutes and had many foreshocks and aftershocks. The area around Quetzaltenango and Sololá was particularly hard hit. Approximately 800 to 900 were killed, and nearly all churches in western Guatemala and eastern Chiapas were heavily damaged or destroyed.
