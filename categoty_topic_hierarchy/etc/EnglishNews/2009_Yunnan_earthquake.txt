The '''2009 Yunnan earthquake''' occurred with a Moment magnitude scale|moment magnitude of 5.7 in Yunnan province, People's Republic of China on 9 July.

== Background and tectonics ==

At 11:19:27 Coordinated Universal Time|UTC (19:19:27 China standard time|local time), a Moment magnitude scale|magnitude 5.7 earthquake with an epicentre at {{coord|25.619|N| 101.086|E|format=dms}} occurred. Eight aftershocks were recorded.

== Damage and casualties ==
The earthquake destroyed 10,000 houses and damaged a further 30,000. One person died
