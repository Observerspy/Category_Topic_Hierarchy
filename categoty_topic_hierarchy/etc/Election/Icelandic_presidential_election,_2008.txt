{{Politics of Iceland}}

An election for the office of [[President of Iceland]] was planned to be held in [[Iceland]] on 28 June 2008.<ref>[http://www.mherrera.org/elections.htm Electoral Calendar- world elections,US elections,presidential election,world parties<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref><!--find a better source than this!--> The incumbent [[Ólafur Ragnar Grímsson]], [[Icelandic presidential election, 1996|first elected in 1996]], stated in his [[New Year]]'s speech that he would contest the election for a fourth term.<ref>[http://english.people.com.cn/90001/90777/90851/6331356.html "Icelandic President wants to be reelected"], Xinhua (''People's Daily Online''), 3 January 2008.</ref> [[Ástþór Magnússon]], who ran unsuccessfully in 1996 and 2004, ruled out a candidacy.<ref>[http://www.mbl.is/mm/frettir/innlent/2008/04/27/astthor_bydur_sig_ekki_fram/ Ástþór býður sig ekki fram - mbl.is<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> No challenger to the incumbent president filed by the deadline to declare a candidacy on 24 May 2008,<ref>http://www.republic.org.nz/node/285</ref> and so Ólafur Ragnar's fourth term was won uncontested.<ref>[http://www.mbl.is/mm/frettir/innlent/2008/05/24/forsetinn_sjalfkjorinn/ Forsetinn sjálfkjörinn - mbl.is<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> He was sworn in on 1 August 2008.<ref>[http://english.people.com.cn/90001/90777/90853/6465286.html "President of Iceland sworn in for fourth time"], Xinhua (''People's Daily Online''), 3 August 2008.</ref>
