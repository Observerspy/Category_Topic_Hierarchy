The '''2012 Kamaishi earthquake''' occurred near the city of Kamaishi, Japan, on December 7.

== Damage ==

=== Tsunami ===
The {{convert|1|m|ft}} tsunami caused a mountain of sandbags to collapse in Ishinomaki, Miyagi|Ishinomaki. An old man went missing and was found dead on December 14 after he sailed out to sea to try to evacuate from the tsunami in Kuji, Iwate|Kuji.

=== Shaking ===
Some pipeworks were damaged in an old people's facility in Saitama, Saitama|Saitama. An old lady died of Shock (circulatory)|shock at her home in Marumori, Miyagi|Marumori, and a man died of shock after trying to evacuate from where he was working in Iitate, Fukushima|Iitate, causing a total of 2 deaths. This earthquake also caused 14 injuries.
